FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 9/4/2025


It was beautiful outside today. The winds laid down a bit, and the sun was out! We had reports of blues, some small pompano and flounder, and a few Spanish this afternoon. Part of our Frank and Fran's crew, Stephanie and Randy, caught some nice fish today! They have been fishing at ramp 32 and catching blues on the bottom with mullet, and caught the Spanish with a pink/white stingsilver.

We got another load of mullet today, and while they are larger than what we had at the beginning of this week, it seems the blues don't mind! Come and get some before they are gone!
