FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 5/29/26


It's been a good day to get ready to kick off the weekend! Today we have reports of lots of bluefish chomping out there on the ocean and sound side. We also saw Jim here in the shop today to weigh in his 1 lb 12 oz sea mullet. Nice catches, everyone! We've got plenty of fresh cob mullet and larger mullet for the weekend!
