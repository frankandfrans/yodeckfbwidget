FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 11/29/25


It's warmed up some, but it's been slow fishing today. Most of our reports have been of scattered sea mullet and sharks, but we did hear of a couple of puppy drum in Buxton this morning. Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) sent us some photos from a charter he had earlier this week; they caught some specks, sheepshead, and black drum! Looks like a fun day!
We still have lots of fresh cobs on deck, and plenty of fresh shrimp! Come grab some to try your luck for the drum and sea mullet, or grab some of our hand-made shark rigs to reel in those sharks that are biting.
