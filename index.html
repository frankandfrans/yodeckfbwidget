FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 7/17/25


Its been a sunny but pretty windy day here on the island. The wind has made fishing pretty hard. We did hear of a few sheepshead up on the Bonner. The Rodanthe area had some croaker and few bluefish. Here in Avon we had lots of croaker. Avon pier had spanish and blues last night. The sound side is still doing well with blues and some trout after sunset. The surf is still seeing sharks at night, although the past 2 nights have been a bit slower. Capt Joey Van Dyke on the Fingeance had a nice catch of blues, some trout and a few puppy drum.
