FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 6/16/2025


It was a nice day out there, minus the short pop-up storm in the middle. There are still plenty of croakers out there, up and down the island. We have had reports of some nice sea mullet mixed in. Sarah and Grace Lynne both got one! We also heard of a few scattered blues on the beach. There have been a couple of speckled trout on the soundside, along with blues and puppy drum. Rick got a nice 25" speck today that was released, and Captain Rick and his crew got some nice blues and spanish.

We stocked up on Frisky Fins today, including their knocker and swing jigs, perfect for sheepshead. Along with the rattle rigs, which make for a great all-purpose rig for the surf that'll catch sea mullet, pompano, croaker, puppy drum, and more!

We are open 6am-9pm daily, or check us out online at www.hatteras-island.com.
