FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 8/10/2025


A little sprinkle of rain here and there today, but overall a nice one! We heard of some small pompano, spot, and sea mullet today. There were also some blues and spanish this morning in Frisco. Oliver got in on the action and landed some nice fish!

We are stocked up on sting silvers/glass minnows to target those early morning spanish bites. Or, if getting up early and active style fishing isn't your thing, we have a variety of Fishbites in stock. They are the easiest bait out there and are productive as well.

We are stocked up on all baits except for fresh mullet. We are expecting that tomorrow afternoon. As always, we will post as soon as we get it in. Come and see us from 6am-9pm or check out our website anytime at www.hatteras-island.com.
