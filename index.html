FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 11/16/25


Turned out to be a beautiful day on the island but with some strong winds on the beach, however, it seems it didn't affect the current. Folks who were able to handle the wind were catching sharks and sea mullet. Gary came in to have his citation 1lb 12oz sea mullet weighed in and Brandon shared some photos catches he made with friends.

Tomorrow looks to be another beautiful day, but with slightly cooler temps. Temps will begin to rise throughout the week, with Friday forecasted to be 67!
