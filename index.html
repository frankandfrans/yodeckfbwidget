FRANKA ND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 6/29/25


It has been another warm day but less humid, the breeze has been out of the SW around 15mph. On the Northern beaches, we heard of a few spanish, blues, croaker, spot, and sea mullet. Last night, there were several spinners and blacktips caught here in Avon. Avon Pier had sharks, blues, and croakers. Down in Frisco we heard of spot and croaker. The sound side continues to produce trout, blues and drum, with better results at night. Sammie caught a very nice spot down on Frisco Beach. We got a fresh cigar restock, with a new additions.
