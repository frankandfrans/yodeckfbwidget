FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/6/25


Another day with not too much wind, but a pretty good amount of fish. Once again, bluefish have been scattered around the island, as well as some flounder and pompano. Soundside has been producing some red drum inshore, flounder, and bluefish, with some speckled trout mixed in here and there. Our own Randy caught a nice 19" bluefish today on a finger mullet rig; Captain Frank Hopkins (Easy Going Charters) had a great day on the boat with Brad, who caught a 35 inch red on a light trout rod with a Penn 3000. Nice work!
