FRANK AND FRNS FISHING REPORT 6/6/25

It has been a great day here on the island. SW wind with blue skies. The fishing was a little lackluster. We heard of bluefish here in Avon. The Point had reports of spanish and blues. Down in Hatteras reports of sea mullet, spanish, and blues. The soundside is producing blues and puppy drum. Brandon and his family found some blues in the sound. Capt. Rick Scarbourg on the Speck-tackler caught a mess of spanish and false albacore. We are open daily from 6am-9pm and always open online @ https://hatteras-island.com #hookedonfandf #fishingreport
