FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 10/23/25


It's been another gorgeous day, and we have reports of sea mullet, bluefish, and red drum from Rodanthe down to Buxton. Haven't heard of any drum south of Buxton so far, but plenty of sea mullet and blues. Brian caught a nice drum on the soundside yesterday, and Alan caught some blues here in Avon late this morning. Captain Joey VanDyke of Fingeance Sportfishing Charters had a great day with his charter out on the water, pulling in blues, red drum, black drum, sheepshead, and some sea bass. Nice catches, y'all!
The NCBBA Tournament is in full swing, and the latest results we have are posted in the photos. Come stop by and see us! We're open every day from 6am-9pm, and you can follow us online at www.hatteras-island.com #fishingreport #hookedonfandf
