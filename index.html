FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 5/1/26


It was another cloudy and windy day with some showers here and there, but the fish are around. We heard of some puppy drum on Avon Pier today, as well as off the beach from down in Hatteras up to Avon. Robert caught some nice puppy drum today, and caught a striper when he went up to Corolla! Nice!
Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler Charters) and his group had a great catch of bluefish and sheepshead. Pretty work!
Tomorrow looks to be a bit nasty, but we are stocked up on fresh mullet for the weekend, and Sunday should be pretty, so come and get some bait and head out to the beach! We are open daily from 6am-7pm, and you can follow us online at www.hatteras-island.com
