FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 5/30/25

The morning started with thunderstorms, and it seems it will end the same way. Currently have wind gusting in the mid-20s out of the wsw. Between the rain and wind, some fish were caught. Here in Avon, we heard of spot and croaker. The Point had a few puppy drum, blues and spanish. The Billy Mitchell area had seamullet, as did ramp 55. The sound has had blues, puppy drum, and trout.
Ben and his friends caught some trout and blues out in the sound. Josh found a nice puppy drum out on the Point. The Smiths, Max and Emily, found some spanish at the Point, Emily's first, but not last.
