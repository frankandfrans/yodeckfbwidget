FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/11/25


We started out today with a few showers and some clouds but it's cleared up some as the day has gone on. Wind is blowing pretty good, and lots of 6oz+ weights are going out the door. Today we've heard of a Spanish bite in Buxton, some red drum scattered through Avon and Buxton, blues up and down the island and some sea mullet. Soundside is producing bluefish, drum, and flounder. Captain Joey VanDyke (Fingeance Sportfishing) had a good charter today, loading up on blues and catching and releasing several above slot drum. Nice work!
