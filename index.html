FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/17/25


The wind has calmed and given way to calmer water, but most of the only reports we heard of fish today were on the piers, which were catching bluefish and some drum. Captain Joey VanDyke (Fingeance Sportfishing) put his charter onto some nice blues, and Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) has been having some luck with reds along with blues. We have fresh shrimp available, frozen tuna belly for the drum, and a variety of other frozen bait, such as mullet and squid. Come in and grab what you need to catch the big one! We are open daily from 6am-9pm, and you can shop with us online at www.hatteras-island.com
