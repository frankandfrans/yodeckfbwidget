FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 8/29/25


Another beautiful day today, with reports of Spanish on the southern beaches, bluefish all over, and some drum in Buxton and the tri-villages. We also heard of small pompano scattered up and down the beach. Captain Joey VanDyke (Fingeance Sportfishing) had a great charter; they caught a mess of Spanish and blues, with Captain Joey saying there was a lot of topwater action. Shelby sent in a picture of Jim and his crew and some nice blues they caught at ramp 43 today. We have plenty of sting silvers and topwater jigs in stock to catch those Spanish Makerel and nice blues that have been around, and lots of fresh mullet and bottom rigs for the drum!
