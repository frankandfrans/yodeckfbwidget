FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 5/29/25


We had a gorgeous day here today; lots of sunshine and light winds. Looks to be showers/storms overnight tonight, but will clear off tomorrow, although as the day goes on, winds will be picking up out of the SSW to around 10-15mph in the afternoon and up to 20mph during the late evening. Today we have reports of scattered sea mullet, croaker, and blues, and heard of some pompano down south of Avon. Our own Randy B. caught and released a flounder this afternoon at Ramp 38, and Captain Joey VanDyke of Fingeance Sportfishing had a great charter, putting them onto lots of blues and Spanish! We have plenty of sting silvers, spoons, and Got-Cha Plugs for the blues and Spanish out there. Come see us and stock up.
