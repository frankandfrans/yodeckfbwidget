FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 8/16/25


It's getting rougher out there! It's taking heavy weight (usually sputnik sinkers) to hold bottom. We have reports once again of bluefish scattered about on the ocean side, and some pompano down at Ramp 55. Soundside is producing some blues and red drum. Jim Price with Psycho Surf Fishing caught a 2.75lb pompano on Ramp 55, and Rick Black got a big 48" red last night back on the sound side! Nice work, guys!
We are stocked up on sputnik sinkers, so if you're fishing in the next few days, come and get a few!
