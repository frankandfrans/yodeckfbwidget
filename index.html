FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 5/25/2025


Beautiful day out there today with reports of blues and Spanish on lures at the point, some sea mullet at 55 and 32, croaker north of Avon, and a few small sharks. There were also a few blues on the soundside.

Two of the shop's owners, Heather and Tim, went offshore today and caught seven nice dolphins before 9:45 this morning! They will be eating well tonight! Mike sent us a picture of our robo bluefish sticker in Ohio today... we love to see everyone #hookedonfandf, even when they aren't here! Captain Rick is still doing well, catching lots of puppy drum and a few specks during his trips.

The wind will pick up tonight into tomorrow morning with a high chance of rain in the morning, clearing off in the afternoon. Come see us in the morning for some rain gear so you can stay dry, or to stock up on everything you need to get out there once it clears off!
