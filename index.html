FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 10/8/25


The surf cleared up enough to produce some smaller fish today up and down the beach, including sea mullet, spot, and some small pompano. Soundside has had some blues and puppy drum here and there. Brian caught a puppy drum on fresh bunker from here at the shop yesterday. Jane and Tom caught some spot and small pompano here in Avon, and Captain Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) and his charter had a good day on the water, catching drum, sheepshead, and blues.
