FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 8/11/2025


The weather is warming up, and the SW wind is on the way and will be here for a few days. Jessica caught a nice puppy drum,her first, that was tagged in Buxton today! If you are interested in learning about what the tags are for, check out https://www.deq.nc.gov/about/divisions/marine-fisheries/science-and-statistics/fish-tagging-program#HowtoReportaTag-4394

There have been reports of spanish and blues in Frisco today, all on lures. We heard that some were caught in the morning, as well as at midday. We didn't hear of much going on in the sound today, but Captain Rick and his crew caught some blues and a nice spanish! They had never fished before, but after a quick lesson, they landed these fish on lures. Nice fish, everyone!

We loaded up on mullet this morning! We also have plenty of shrimp, bloodworms, and a variety of frozen baits in stock. Come and see us from 6am-9pm any day of the week.
