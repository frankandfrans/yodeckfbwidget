FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 6/27/25


We cooled down today from yesterday, the heat index only hit 107 deg. There have been a few fish to catch, but it's mainly a sunrise, sunset, and nighttime bite. We heard of spanish and blues here in Avon this morning. During the day, it has mainly been small croaker with a few sea mullet mixed in. Down in Hatteras, we got reports of a few pompano. The inlet water temp was 89 deg today. The soundside at night is still doing well with drum and blues. Oceanside at night we are hearing of more and more sharks. We have Chase with a cool lookdown. Joe, with some nicer bluefish, believe he is in Duck. Capt Rick keeps finding the spanish and blues.
