FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 04/27/2026


Brrr, thank goodness it'll be a bit warmer tomorrow! We went from the 80s and sunshine to the 50s and howling wind. Amy and her crew caught some nice ones in Avon today. Kevin and Chris got in on the action at 43 yesterday afternoon. Bruce caught a nice 33" at the point yesterday on his new Tica rod paired with an Okuma Avenger reel he picked up on Saturday. And last but not least, Trip, Doug, and Mitch from South Carolina went out with Captain Rick and caught their share of blues.
