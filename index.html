FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 5/12/25

It has been overcast and looking like it could rain at any minute, but has not, yet. At the Bonner bridge, we heard of big blues and a few sheepshead. Here in Avon we heard of blues, sea mullet, pompano, and even a few spanish mackerel. The Point area had great overnight bluefish, a few blues in the daylight, and a handful of puppy drum. Down south at ramp 49, we heard of a few sea mullet. Ramp 55 reported a few blues and lots of grass. The sound side is still doing well with blues and puppy drum. Jack went out on the Miss Hatteras yesterday and reported good fishing, including this 20# amber jack. Joshua caught a nice puppy drum at ramp 25.
