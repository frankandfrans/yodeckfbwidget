FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 7/20/2025


It has been a warm one out there today, breeze out of the southwest around 15-20mph. Here in Avon, we have heard of an abundance of croaker, a few blues, and some sea mullet mixed in. Buxton reported the same. Seems the water is clearer once you hit the south beaches, as we heard of a few pompano, blues and spanish from Frisco into Hatteras. For those wanting to tangle with sharks, we have been getting steady reports of spinners, blacktips, sandtigers, and a few hammerheads mixed in at night. Discovery Channel's Shark Week starts tonight if you want to watch some cool videos. Michele caught some nice pompano fishing down south on her Ninja Dagger using a Frank & Fran's Sand Flea Imposter rig and Ez - Flea Fishbites.
