FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 6/25/26


The clouds took over this evening, but thankfully, it's keeping things a bit cooler. Today, we heard of some sea mullet scattered around, and some blues and Spanish mackerel in Buxton. We also weighed in a citation pompano this morning! Soundside has been producing blues and drum.

Luke caught a 26.5" red on sand fleas this morning, casting close in to the shore. Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) has put his charter onto lots of reds, too. And Beth, Chuck, and Haley had a half-day charter up near Pea Island the other day, and caught sharks and triggerfish. Nice catches!
