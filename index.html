FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 4/4/26


What a great start to Easter weekend! Today, we have reports of sea mullet on the north end of the island as well as some blowtoads, and puppy drum scattered north and south. Pretty work, y'all! We have lots of fresh mullet left for the rest of the weekend, and are well stocked on rigs, lead, and just about anything else you may need!
