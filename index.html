FISHING REPORT FOR FRANK AND FRANS 5/7/25

It has been a splendid day on the island today. Sunny with light and variable winds. Overall the fish seemed pretty spread out. In the Avon area we heard of some big blues, puppy drum, and sea mullet. The Point area had a few pompano and big bluefish. The southern beaches had pompano, sea mullet, bluefish and a spanish or two. Lots of great fishing photos today. Frank with a real nice bluefish in Avon. Janice with a stud pompano. Jim the maker of Psycho Rigs caught a MONSTER 5# 3oz Pompano on his own rig The Legs and Eggs. Michael had a nice blue and drum. Gabby caught a nice bluefish as well. Curtis wasnt going to be left out, he found a good bluefish too. Mike, Alex, Sean and Priscilla all from Vermout got in on the bluefish action while visiting this week. Yall killed it.
