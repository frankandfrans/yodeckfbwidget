FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 4/9/26


It's been another windy one, but some sunshine peeked through the clouds and warmed things up a little more than yesterday. The drum love this rough surf, and we have reports of puppy drum here in Avon at ramps 34 and 38, and reports from the point of puppy drum and sharks. Ashley sent in a picture of Axel's 29" red drum caught on mullet this morning at ramp 38, and we also got a photo from Paul of his first puppy drum from south of the Avon Pier! Nice catches! Thank you for sharing!
