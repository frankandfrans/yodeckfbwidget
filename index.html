FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING 6/13/26


It's been a hot one out there, today! We've got reports of smaller fish such as spot, croaker, and sea mullet up and down the beach, and bluefish have been scattered as well. There was a report of pompano at Ramp 55 in Hatteras, and Spanish Mackerel have been cruising from Avon down to Hatteras. Soundside has been continuously producing bluefish, red drum, speckled trout, and some sheepshead. David D. and his wife Cindy caught a load of Spanish on Ramp 38 a couple of days ago, and David M. got one today! Doug got a 25" bluefish on the soundside using cut mullet he got from us here at the shop. Nice catches, everyone! Thanks for sharing with us!
