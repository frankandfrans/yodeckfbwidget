FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 10/21/2025


It is AWESOME to see the photos rolling in today! Lots of drum, some blues, sea mullet, a few black drum, and flounder have been caught today. Pretty work to all today! We love to see you all having luck out there and catching some beautiful fish

We ended up getting about 1300 lbs of mullet today, which is quickly diminishing! We will continue to load up for the tournament as much as we can.
