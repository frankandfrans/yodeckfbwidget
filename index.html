FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 6/1/25


It has been a beautiful day here on the island, with light winds and plenty of sun. We have heard too much today, but that's probably because everyone is on the beach. Here in Avon, we heard of blues and croaker. The Point area had blues and a few spanish. Hatteras Inlet area had some very nice pompano. The soundside here in Avon had some pups and nice blues on topwater. Adam caught a 4# 1oz pompano down at 55, a very nice fish. Capt Joey Van Dyke of the Fingeance lead his clients to a nice catch of blues today. We have several days of SE wind coming up, look for clear water and pompano and spanish fishing to be good.
