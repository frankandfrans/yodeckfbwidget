FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 5/13/25

It rained and then it rained more and the wind was blowing pretty strong out of the SE most of the morning. About 4pm, the sun came out, but the ocean remains rough. We haven't heard much as most anglers are just now starting to make it out. Would expect the blue fish to still be present, fingers crossed, the stirred up surf causes the drum to return to the beach. SE wind causes the water to clear up so we might see a few spanish around. Anderson makes another appearance on the report with a nice Hammerhead he caught at the Point, Shawn had a great trip with a 33" drum and a 35" bluefish. Ralph found a pretty drum in the sound, he caught it on his trout rod in 2 ft of water. Pretty work. Captain Scarborough took his clients out on the Speck-tackler and they found some good eating Sheepshead.
