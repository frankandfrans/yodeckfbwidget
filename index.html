FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 10/7/2025


We have a couple more days before it gets nasty out there! Today was a nice day, although there was a lot of cloud cover. The Avon Pier did well again with drum this morning. We heard of a few pompano, spot, and sea mullet down at ramp 55. We also heard of a few puppy drum at ramp 38 and north of Avon on the soundside. Jane and Tom got into a pinfish blitz on the soundside today! If you head that way, make sure to take plenty of mosquito repellent!

We are loaded up on fresh mullet, but it is going fast. Come and get it before it is gone! Open 6am-9pm daily.
