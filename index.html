FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 8/27/25


We had a few short, pop-up showers here in Avon earlier this afternoon, but other than that, it's been a sunny and beautiful day. We have reports of bluefish on the surf and sound sides, along with some below-slot puppy drum on the sound side. We have also heard of a good-sized trout on the sound last night.
Captain Sam Bell (Tightlinez Fishing Charters) put today's charter onto some blues and puppy drum, saying it was a "fun day with a great group of guys." Pretty work! We have fresh finger mullet and a few cobbs mixed in that came in earlier today, so grab some while it lasts! We are open daily from 6am-9pm, and you can follow us and shop online at www.hatteras-island.com
