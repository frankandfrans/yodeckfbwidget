FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 5/2/26


Today was chilly and rainy, but those who braved the weather and rough surf caught some puppy drum and bluefish up and down the island. Tomorrow the clouds will break, and hopefully fishing will continue to get better and better! Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) had another productive charter, putting them on to loads of blues and sheepshead! Looks like it was a great day!
