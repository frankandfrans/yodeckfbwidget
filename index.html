FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 5/6/25

It has been a nice day here on the island with a SE breeze gusting at times. Here in Avon we have heard of puppy drum, a few big blues and sea mullet. At Cape Point there were some big blues, sharks and rays. The Frisco area reported slower fishing, with a couple pompano, and small sea mullet. Hatteras Inlet had some blues, flounder and sea mullet. We sold this young lady a fishing rod this morning and she seems to have already mastered it. Donald found a 2.8 #pompano from somewhere down south, nice fish sir. Matt, the father of the young lady with a flounder from the Point. Jamie also found a citation pompano, I see ceviche in her future. Some good fishing out there.
