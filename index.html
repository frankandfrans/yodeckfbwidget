🛻 Beach Driving Permits
Hitting the sand with your 4x4? Grab your permit online first, then stop by for a FREE self-cert card — just add your permit number and display it.
👉 https://www.recreation.gov/vehiclepermits/249978

🔥 Beach Fires
The burn ban has been lifted and beach fires are allowed. We have firewood and the fire permit that is needed. Treat mom to a fire on the beach this Mother's Day!

🎣 Fishing Licenses
Ages 16+ need a NC license — we’ve got you covered in-store.
Quick + easy (email + last 4 of SSN), and it’s sent straight to your inbox.
