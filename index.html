FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 4/25/26


Seems like it was a good start to the 4Plus Tournament today, and a good day for fishing. There have been reports from up and down the beach of sea mullet, bluefish, and black drum, and puppy drum down in Hatteras. Christy sent in some photos of her family's bluefish catches this week! Christy said, "Big thanks to Frank & Fran’s for all the bait and gear this week. Our girl fell in love with surf fishing and landed her first bluefish today off ramp 55." So glad we could help her get into fishing! Thanks for the business and kind comments!
Jeremy sent in a picture of Kevin with his huge ray last night and Ramp 38, and Brian had luck with bluefish, a shark, and a ray at the point. Jim of Psycho Surf Fishing caught a nice black drum at Ramp 55 with his new Psycho Flea rig, and Curtis was there, too, and caught a 31" red drum on the Legs and Eggs rig. Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler Charters) has started off his season on a great note with Joe and Sherri, putting them on to blues and sheepshead, the biggest sheepshead being 13.5 pounds! And Ronnie, David and Jeremy had a great catch of sea mullet today! Nice work, everyone, and thanks for sharing! We are open from 6am-7pm, so come on in and see us.
