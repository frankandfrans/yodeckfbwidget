FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 6/21/2026


Happy Father's Day! It has been a beautiful day with reports of sea mullet, pompano, and some blues. We weighed in a nice 3.17lb citation pomp that Tim caught today on Fishbites, and we saw some others caught as well. Chester had a nice day yesterday with the mullet and a nice pompano.

Fishbites are a great fresh bait alternative that still catch the fish! We carry them in-store and online. You may see we keep them in the fridge while you are in the shop; that is because they last longer this way! While they are not required to be refrigerated, it helps to maintain the bright color. If you have some left at the end of the week, toss them in the fridge or freezer, and they'll last until your next trip! https://hatteras-island.com/search.php?search_query=fishbites
