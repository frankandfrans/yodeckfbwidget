FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 5/11/25

It's been a cloudy, cooler day here on the island with a few drops of rain. The fishing here in Avon has been good; several folks are reporting large bluefish and a few puppy- to yearling-size drums. A few citation-sized sea mullet have also been caught. The Point area has seen big blues, rays, and sharks. The Frisco beach area has been producing sea mullet, blues, and a few small flounder. The only thing I heard from the inlet is big blues. The soundside is producing puppy drum, blues, and some nice trout, though they have to be released. Terry caught a real nice 30" bluefish. Daniel had a cool story to tell, his bottom rig got snagged on a lost rig that was attached to a 25"puppy drum. Pretty cool. Alex and his group went offshore and landed a good mess of tuna and a blue marlin. Mike found a couple of nice puppy drum on the beach here in Avon, they were over slot. Steve and his wife are staying here in Avon and caught some really nice blues on the beach. #hookedonfandf #fishingreport
