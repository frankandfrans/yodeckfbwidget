FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 9/1/2025


Another windy day out there today, but nice and sunny. We heard of blues and sea mullet today. Blues are being caught on bottom rigs with mullet for bait. We have seen a few false albacore caught over the past week or so. This one was caught on a surfboard with light tackle! The soundside produced some blues and puppy drum today.
