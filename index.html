FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/25/25


We had another relatively warm day today, and we have reports of small bluefish, small pompano, and some sea mullet, all of which are scattered along the island. Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) put his charter onto some nice blues and speckled trout, and Rick and Mike went out on the sound and caught a nice bluefish today! Nice work, everyone!
