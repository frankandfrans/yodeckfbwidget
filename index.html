FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/20/25


It got a little windier today but it was very sunny and beautiful. We've heard of bluefish and sharks up and down the island as well as some puppy drum here and there, and some pompano were reported just north of Avon. Soundside has reported bluefish and puppy drum. Yesterday at Ramp 32, Randy and Steve caught a few nice bluefish, and today Randy and Stephanie got a puppy drum, some pompano and some blues. On the soundside here in Avon, Brandon caught a 29" bluefish, and Paul caught his first ever blue, measuring 19". Great catches, everyone! Thanks for sharing!
