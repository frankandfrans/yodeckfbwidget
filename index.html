FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/18/25


It turned out to be a beautiful day, with lots of sunshine for most of the day and clouds this evening. The wind and water were pretty calm, and we have reports of bluefish on the beach from Avon down to Hatteras. Some drum are still being caught on the piers. Soundside is producing nice bluefish and some puppy drum. Lola and her mom, Katie, caught some nice bluefish on the sound today, Lola with a 20" and Katie with a 34"! Nice work! Emily and her crew caught some blues down in Hatteras, and Brian got a nice red off the surf here in Avon last night! Zach and Sabrina went up to Oregon Inlet and caught several sheepshead weighing up to 6.23lbs on sand fleas. Great fish, everyone! We have loads of fresh mullet in, and lots of bluefish and drum rigs. Come grab what you need to catch the big one! We are open daily from 6am-9pm, and you can shop for rigs and more at https://hatteras-island.com/rigs-beads/
