FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 7/10/26


Fishing was slower today than yesterday, but there have still been some spot and croaker biting, along with the sharks on the oceanside. Soundside is mostly producing bluefish during the morning and late evening hours.

Kaylee and Piper were rocking their F&F hats while they were out fishing, and Kaylee caught some spots! Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) and his charter got onto some bluefish, and Captain Joey Van Dyke (Fingeance Sportfishing) and his charter caught some blues, shark, and bowfished for stingrays.
