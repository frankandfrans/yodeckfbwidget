FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 5/2025

It has been a nice sunny day with NE wind. The word of the day is BLUEFISH. The blues have been running bait on the beach up and down the beach here in Avon. Nice fish from 20"-30". Rodanthe Pier reported a 41" drum. Avon Pier has had a great day with blues, sea mullet and spanish. The Point area has reported bluefish and a few puppy drum. Down toward the inlet we heard of a few mullet, blues, and pompano. Eric with his nice 41" drum, Jim caught a nice pompano. Speck Tackler with Capt Rick found grey trout and puppy drum.
