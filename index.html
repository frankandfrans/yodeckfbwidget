FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 10/2/25


Despite the wind and more overwash down in Buxton, it turned out to be a cool, gorgeous day! From the surfside, we didn't have many reports, just a couple of puppy drum in South Buxton. Soundside here in Avon, we heard of nice-sized bluefish and some puppy drum this morning, along with some larger drum last night. When fishing the sound side, if you want to cast out and enjoy sitting for a bit, a double drop bottom rig with some mullet is perfect! If you'd rather be actively jigging, try a popping cork jig! Cast out, and as you're reeling, give it a good tug now and then to bring the fish in! We have all these rigs and more that are pre-tied for your convenience; or you can build your own!
