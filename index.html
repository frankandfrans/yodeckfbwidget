FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 5/15/26


It's been gorgeous outside, if a little windy. There were lots of reports of sea mullet today up and down the beaches. There have even been several citations weighed in in the last couple of days, including this one from Ivie, weighing in at 2.18lbs! Excellent catch! We have also heard of scattered flounder, blues, and puppy drum. Fresh shrimp and Bloodworm and Sand Flea Fishbites have been working for the sea mullet on double-bottom rigs. Fresh mullet is working well for the bluefish and the puppy drum. We have fresh bait in stock, as well as plenty of wire leaders and wire rigs if the blues are giving you trouble and cutting your line. Come see our selection!
