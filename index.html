FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 3/25/26


Thank goodness today was warmer than yesterday! The chilly weather is still coming and going, but fishing is picking up. We have reports of puppy drum today from ramp 34, ramp 49, and from down in Hatteras.

Nice fish, y'all! Thanks for stopping in and sharing! We got more fresh cob mullet in today, we have plenty of fresh shrimp, we are stocked on lead and rigs, and we have just about anything else you'd need to enjoy a day on the beach!
