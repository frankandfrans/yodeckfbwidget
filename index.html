FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 6/2/2026


Whew, it's a bit windy outside! 💨We haven't heard much from the surf today with all the wind. We did hear of some puppy drum from the soundside. Our local charter guys did well. Capt Joey VanDyke did well with blues and drum. Pretty work, y'all!

Capt Buddy did well with drum. He said that Avon soundside fishing has turned on. With a Southwest wind, these fish can be caught right from the soundside on the shore. There is easy access using any of the National Park Service roads between Avon and Salvo. He also said many of these drum were caught in around 2' of water, so you don't always have to look for deep water!
