FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 6/11/25


It's been pretty out, but warm and humid. We've heard of blues, croaker and small sea mullet scattered around everywhere, and there were a few puppy drum here in Avon! The Grimes family has had a great turnout of fish in the last few days, catching pups, blues, and some speckled trout (that were released) on the soundside. On the oceanside, they caught blues, Spanish mackerel, puppy drum, and caught and released small flounder. Ethan (9yo) from Pennsylvania caught a pretty 31" red just north of the Avon Pier, and Lilly got a 3' shark last night! Very nice catches, everyone! Thank you for sharing!
