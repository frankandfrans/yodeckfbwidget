FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 6/14/25

It has been overcast and windy all day; scattered showers have developed in the afternoon. The northern beaches have had croaker and a few sea mullet. Frisco had more and larger mullet, plus a few blues and pompano. Hatteras Inlet was the best of it, pompano and sea mullet made a good showing. James caught a 2# 10oz pompano on one of his own Psycho Rigs https://hatteras-island.com/search.php?search_query=psycho+ Zach caught a nice 33' drum on Kelly's boat. Miss Angela caught and released a flounder last night. Joe picked himself a new pair of rods, thank ya sir. And Capt Scarborough took his crew out and caught cobia and specs. Nice work all.
