FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 6/25/25

If ya like well done, today was the day for you. It's been hot, humid, and there's been a very light breeze. We have heard of spot, croaker, and a few Spanish here in Avon. Frisco reported much of the same. Hatteras had some pompano as well as blues. Curtis caught a nice 2# 8oz pomp down at 55, yep it was caught on a Psycho rig, https://hatteras-island.com/search.php?search_query=psycho+rig. Capt. Rick Scarborough on the Speck-Tackler caught a mess of blues and a nice summer-time cobia. It's been hot, but there are fish to be caught, fish you low light conditions, sunrise and sunset, and see your catch rate go up.
