FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/13/25


The wind is still kicking, and the water is pretty rough; lots of 10 and 12oz weights going out.
There was a good red drum bite at Avon Pier today, and some bluefish and flounder were reported in Buxton.
We got some great pictures from the pier, including this gentleman's first citation drum, and several others over 40" that followed. Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) had a great charter, loading up on blues and a limit of puppy drum. Nice work, everyone, and thanks for sharing!
