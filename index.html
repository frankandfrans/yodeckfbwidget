🛻 Beach Driving Permits
Hitting the sand with your 4x4? Grab your permit online first, then stop by for a FREE self-cert card (just add your permit # and display it).
👉 https://www.recreation.gov/vehiclepermits/249978

🔥 Beach Bonfires
We’ve got firewood bundles with permits included 🔥
Need just a permit? It’s FREE. Don’t forget a fire starter for an easy light-up.

🎣 Fishing Licenses
Ages 16+ need a NC license — we’ve got you covered in-store.
Quick + easy (email + last 4 of SSN), and it’s sent straight to your inbox.

🐟 Fishing Report & Gear
Puppy drum on shrimp and cut mullet, blue fish on cut mullet, blowtoads on bloodworms and shrimp, sea mullet on shrimp and Fishbites.
