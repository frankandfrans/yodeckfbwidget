🌴 Welcome to Hatteras Island! 🌊


A big thanks to this week’s visitors — we hope you had a blast — and a warm welcome to our new island friends! 🐠☀️ Whether you're here to fish, relax, or soak up that salty breeze, Frank & Fran’s Fisherman’s Friend is here to help you make the most of it. Here’s what you need to know:

🛻 Beach Driving Permits
Planning to cruise the beach in your 4x4? Grab your permit online:
👉 https://www.recreation.gov/vehiclepermits/249978
Then swing by Frank & Fran's for a free self-cert card — fill it out and pop it in your windshield. Easy!

🔥 Bonfires on the Beach? Yes Please!
We’ve got firewood bundles including permit. Just need a permit? We’ll hook you up. And don’t forget fire starters!

🎣 Fishing Licenses
Anyone 16+ needs a NC fishing license. We sell ‘em in-store. With the new system, we’ll need your email + last 4 of your SSN. Fast, easy, and sent straight to your inbox.

🦀 Crabbing Fun for the Whole Crew
We’ve got chicken necks, nets, pots, and lines. The sound is full of crabs — and crabbing’s a blast for kids and adults alike! 🦀

🐟 Fishing Report & Gear
Croaker, spot, bluefish, Spanish mackerel, sea mullet, pompano, and sharks are being caught.
Spanish & blues love Stingsilvers, Gotcha plugs & glass minnows.
Shrimp and double drop bottom rig for the small fish.
Mullet and a shark rig to snag a shark.
In the sound, trout & puppy drum are biting on popping corks, soft plastics & fresh mullet on a slider rig — especially in the evenings.

🎒 New to Fishing?
Grab a Starter Kit: rod, reel, sand spike, rig, bait, sinker & a 5-gal bucket. Just add a license and hit the beach!
