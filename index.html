FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 4/15/26


There's been a good bit of wind today, but other than that, it's been a gorgeous day. Sea mullet, sharks, and skates have been seen all up and down the island today, as well as a flounder. Our own Stephanie and Randy went out and caught some sea mullet, dogsharks, and skates, and Stephanie caught and released a nice flounder. Randy and Bill of the Virginia Beach Angler's Club came down yesterday and hit Ramp 34 before it closed, catching two red drum one 35" and one 26", and later a 22" bluefish. And Adam & Ariel had a good day out with Buddy O'Neal, catching 6 bluefish & a 35 inch red drum. Thank you, everyone, for sharing! Nice catches! We've got plenty of fresh mullet, cob and larger-sized, and that seems to be what most fish are biting on at the moment, along with fresh shrimp.
We are open daily from 7am-7pm, and you can follow us and shop online at www.hatteras-island.com
