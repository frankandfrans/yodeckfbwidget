FRANK & FRANS HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/26/25


Another warm day today, but a nice day nonetheless! Today we have reports of bluefish, croaker, spot, and sea mullet scattered around the island, along with some puppy drum and blowtoads here in Avon. ON the sound side, folks are catching blues, puppy drum and speckled trout. Will sent us a photo of an octopus they found today! Very neat! And Matt sent us a photo of his puppy drum he caught on the sound! Nice work!

We just got in another load of mullet; cobs and some bigger pound-and-a-halfers, and we still have a few fingers left as well. Come see us and grab some. We are open every day from 6am-9pm, and you can shop with us and follow us online at www.hatteras-island.com
