FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 5/23/25


It's still pretty warm out but has cooled off just a touch; winds will be coming from the NNW late tonight and switching to come primarily from the West tomorrow. Today we have reports of a few blues and spanish on Avon Pier, and scattered blues, sea mullet and sharks up and down the island. Cadan and Kindra caught some nice bluefish on Ramp 38 this morning using metal. Captain Rick Scarborough of the Speck-Tackler had a good charter catching speckled trout, blues and puppy drum, and this evening Beau caught a citation sea mullet. Pretty fish, everyone, and thanks for sharing! Come see us this weekend and grab what you need to get out there and catch some fish!
