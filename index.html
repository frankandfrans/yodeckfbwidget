FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 6/7/25

It's been a warm one on the island today. Pretty muggy out there. The fishing has been pretty good too. Here in Avon we heard of citation sea mullet, bluefish, and a few pompano. The Point area has had some bluefish and spanish. Down in Hatteras, we heard of blues, sea mullet, and pompano. The soundside has had good bluefish and puppy drum.
