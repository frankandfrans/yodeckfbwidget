FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 10/15/25


We had some showers to start off the day today; they cleared up, but the clouds stuck around. We got reports of puppy drum on the surf in Buxton and on the pier here in Avon, and of a black drum off the surf as well. There have also been sea mullet scattered around. Soundside has been quiet today.
