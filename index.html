FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/19/25


Another fine day of heading towards the fall season! Today, we have reports of bluefish scattered up and down the beach, with a good bite earlier in Buxton, followed by some red drum. There was a Spanish bite this morning here in Avon, and some sizeable pompano in Hatteras. Those fishing soundside have reported bluefish, puppy drum, and speckled trout today. Curtis caught a 3lb 12oz pompano on Ramp 55 today using a Psycho Surf Fishing Legs and Eggs rig. Michael posted some photos of some nice blues and drum, and Brandon got a 23" bluefish in the sound here in Avon. Amy sent in some pictures of Rich with his catches today; he caught and released some 24" drum on Ramp 27 with some mullet from our shop! Tony sent in some pictures from his vacation here, where he caught his first flounder and trout! Nice fish, y'all! Thanks for sharing!
