🎣 FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT – 10/25/2025 🎣

What. A. Week. Hatteras Island was alive with excitement as anglers from the Capital City Four Wheeler Tournament overlapped with NCBBA competitors, long-time fall fishing crews, and a wave of new visitors chasing BIG fish!

🏆 NCBBA TOURNAMENT WRAP-UP
Today marked the finale of the 16th Annual North Carolina Beach Buggy Association Red Drum Tournament, proudly hosted here at Frank & Fran’s!
A HUGE thank you to the NCBBA volunteers, judges, sponsors, bait fisherman—and of course, YOU, the anglers, who make this one of the most anticipated surf-fishing events on the East Coast. After a quick power nap... we will start gearing up for next year! 🔥

📸 Catch Highlights

🐟 Glen pulled in a 2 lb 9 oz sea mullet so thick it looked like a puppy drum at first glance!

🐟 Travis wasn’t far behind with a 2 lb 6 oz sea mullet — fall fishing is HEATING UP.

🦈 Thomas battled a nice shark.
