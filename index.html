FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 6/28/2026


A mixture of rain and shine today on the island. We had reports of blues, spanish, croaker, sea mullet, and sharks today. The Stout family got it done!!!! One pole got dragged by a shark about 60 yards into the water, and ran into the other poles' drum rig. We were able to fight the shark for 1.5 hours, saved the pole, and brought the shark to shore. Quickly and safely released the shark. Congrats on the catch and glad the rod came back with it!!
