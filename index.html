FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 6/7/25

It's been a warm one on the island today. Pretty muggy out there. The fishing has been pretty good too. Here in Avon we heard of citation sea mullet, bluefish, and a few pompano. The Point area has had some bluefish and spanish. Down in Hatteras, we heard of blues, sea mullet, and pompano. The soundside has had good bluefish and puppy drum. Don caught a nice 31" puppy he was guided by Kelly. Wayne found a just under 2# pompano down in Hatteras-Carter caught a nice sea mullet and croaker, nicely done.
