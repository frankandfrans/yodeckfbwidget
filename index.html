FRANK AND FRANS HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 6/23/2025


It was a hot one out there today! We have heard of lots of croaker and a few sea mullet on the Surf side today, and some speckled trout in the sound. Speckled trout will finally be opening soon, on July 1st, with a slot limit of 14-20 inches with one fish over 26", and a total bag limit of 3 fish per day per angler.

Adam caught a pretty pompano in Hatteras at ramp 55, and Rick got a 29.5" speckled trout on his boat today! Nice work y'all
