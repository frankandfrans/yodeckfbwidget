FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 4/3/26


What a gorgeous day, with more good fishing, too! We have reports of scattered sea mullet and puppy drum from both the north and south ends of the island, and black drum here in Avon. Jason sent in a photo of Konnor's 32" red on ramp 34 today, and Robert and his son James fished around north Avon today, and James caught his first ever red drum, measuring 27"! Rachel sent some pictures of Coleman and Kevin, fishing from ramp 23, saying, "Coleman and Kevin caught red drum, black drum, and as many whiting as you wanted to pull out of the ocean with fresh shrimp and Fish Bites purchased from Frank and Frans!"
We also got pictures of Beth's first-ever red drum, caught on shrimp at ramp 27, and Ronnie and his crew caught some nice sea mullet and a blowtoad on shrimp. Pretty work, everyone! Thanks for sharing!
