FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - Extended Edition - 6/22/2025

Wonder why it's the extended edition? Well, our friends and family are catching some great mullet and shark down on Edisto using the Frank & Fran's Sand Flea Imposter rig! This rig features two drops with small floats that mimic sand fleas and size 1 hooks. Hand-tied right here in Avon, but they work everywhere! Thanks to Shayne and Jenna for sharing their catches with us from Edisto Island, SC.

Meanwhile, locals Dale and Mike are doing great in the Pamlico Sound with catches of puppy drum and bluefish! Both safely released!
