FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 11/6/25


The Angler's Club tournament is in full swing! It's been pretty windy today, but the wind is supposed to die down some and switch to the south tomorrow. Today we have reports of sea mullet up and down the beaches, and bluefish here in Avon, up in the Tri-Villages, in Buxton, and in Hatteras. There have also been some red drum reported along the beaches. Tommy caught a nice drum on the point yesterday! Nice catch!
We have plenty of fresh mullet on deck, and lots of fresh shrimp, frozen squid, tuna belly, and more! Come in and stock up on what you need for day two of the tournament! We are open every day from 6am-7pm, and you can shop with us online at www.hatteras-island.com #fishingreport #hookedonfandf
