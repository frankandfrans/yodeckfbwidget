Frank & Fran's Kid's Fishing Tournament - Big thanks to @thesurffishingguy for being a tournament sponsor!


We announced our winners for this week but we have lots of great photos from other young anglers to share.

Warren Miller, Age 12, caught 21 fish this week. He fished in the surf, sound and off the pier. He caught a wide variety: Pig Fish, Spot, Blues, Lizard fish, Whiting, Drum, Flounder, and Pin Fish. He went out with Patrick Kurtz on Last Cast Charter in Hatteras and caught some Drum & Blues. His biggest fish this week was caught on the charter, a 30” Red Drum. His largest catch from the beach was an 11.5” Blue Fish. that he landed using a Top/Bottom Rig and shrimp for bait. Love that smile!

Aria Kroner, age 12, landed three speckled trout today. Her largest was 19”. Way to go Aria!
