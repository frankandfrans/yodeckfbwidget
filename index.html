Whether you’re here to fish, relax, or chase that salty breeze, Frank & Fran’s Fisherman’s Friend has everything you need to make it unforgettable. Here’s your quick island starter guide 👇

🛻 Beach Driving Permits
Hitting the sand with your 4x4? Grab your permit online first, then stop by for a FREE self-cert card — just add your permit number and display it.
👉 https://www.recreation.gov/vehiclepermits/249978

🔥 Beach Fires
Please note: there is currently a statewide burn ban in effect, so beach fires are not allowed at this time. Maybe this rain will help!

🎣 Fishing Licenses
Ages 16+ need a NC license — we’ve got you covered in-store.
Quick + easy (email + last 4 of SSN), and it’s sent straight to your inbox.

🐟 Fishing Report & Gear
Puppy drum on shrimp and cut mullet, bluefish on cut mullet or whole finger mullet, sea mullet on shrimp and Fishbites, plus it is time for pompano and we have peeler crabs, sand fleas and Fishbites. Drum Slider rigs, Psycho Legs & Eggs, Psycho Cherry Bomb, and the Frank & Fran’s Sand Flea Imposter are all getting the job done!
