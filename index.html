FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 7/15/26


It sounds like there were mostly smaller fish like croaker out there today, but mixed in we had some blues, sharks, and rays on the ocean side, and soundside had blues, speckled trout, and red drum.

Captain Joey Van Dyke (Fingeance Sportfishing) took out Robert and his group and caught lots of nice bluefish. Captain Frankie Hopkins (Easygoing Charters) took out a group and caught some drum, blues, and a Spanish. Harris sent in photos of Lucy and Graydon with their speckled trout caught soundside, and Bill and Casey caught croaker and sea mullet at ramp 27 on sand flea fishbites. Last night, Nick caught and safely released a 62" shark on bonita, and Christie and Cayson caught sharks and rays at the point today. Nice work, everyone! Thanks for sharing!
