FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 5/28/26


The wind was certainly more forgiving today than it was yesterday. We started off with some thunder showers this morning, but it has cleared up to be a pretty day. We heard of scattered sea mullet and red drum today, as well as a few small sharks. Last night on the soundside, Dustin caught his personal best red drum, measuring 30", and Wendy sent in some pictures of Lathum (8y/o) and Brad, with 44" and 49" drum. Very nice fish, everyone! We have lots of fresh shrimp in stock, and rigs for just about any fish you can catch out there! Come see us!
