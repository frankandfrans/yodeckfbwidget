FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/12/25


It was a windy day today, and it still takes a lot of weight to hold out there. We heard of some red drum on the Avon Pier, bluefish up and down the beach, and some flounder and drum on the soundside. Joe caught a nice flounder today, and Matthew came in to weigh in his flounder. It weighed in at 6.09lbs and measured 23.25"
