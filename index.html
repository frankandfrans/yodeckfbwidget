FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 7/15/2025


The weather was decent for most of the day, minus the downpour midday. Similar reports to yesterday. A ton of croaker, a few sea mullet and blues, and some blues and Spanish on gotcha plugs at the pier. Of course, some sharks and rays are out there as well.
