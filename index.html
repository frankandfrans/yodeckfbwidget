FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT 7/11/25

Its been a warm day with a NW wind. Fishing has been a bit slow, unless you're into croaker. Here in Avon we heard of croaker, a few nice sea mullet and scattered bluefish. Also the shark fishing has been pretty good here in Avon. Buxton reported more of the same. Hatteras had more blues and croakers. The sound side has been the best of it with blues, trout and puppy drum. Axel caught a STUD sea mullet at 18.5" We just got a Turtlebox restock, they rock. Fresh mullet just hit the deck as well.
