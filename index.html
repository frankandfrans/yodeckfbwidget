FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 8/7/25


It's been increasingly windy today and mostly cloudy, and the water has been pretty rough. We've had word of sea mullet here in Avon today, but other than that, it's been mostly quiet. Soundside has had reports of puppy drum in the evenings and at night. Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) put his charter on some speckled trout! Nice work, guys!
