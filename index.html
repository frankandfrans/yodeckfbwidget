FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 5/24/25


It was a beautiful day here today, with light winds and scattered fish up and down the island. We heard of sheepshead and pompano further south down the island, scattered blues and Spanish, and scattered sea mullet. Soundside has had reports of some blues and small flounder that were caught and released today. We received a picture from Mandy of Larry and his catches from Avon Pier on Friday evening, some nice Spanish caught on a Got-Cha plug. Sandy caught a nice pompano today, and Jim Price of Psycho Surf Fishing Rigs caught up with some friends, saying, "Ricky with the nice 8 and 1/2 lb Sheepshead. Fishing right next to me using the sand flea legs and eggs rig. Lots of nice sea mullet and Tammy with the pretty Pompano."
Pretty work, everyone!
