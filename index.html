FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 5/8/25

It has been a nice day here on the island the fishing has been pretty good too. Here in Avon we have had sea mullet, a few puppy drum and big blues. The Point area has had big blues, a few puppy drum and all the rays and sharks you could ever want. The inlet has had a few pompano and a nice sheepshead. The sound side has had a few larger trout(released), blues and puppy drum. Jake and Jordan weighed in a few nice citation sheepshead, from an inshore boat.Jerry caught a real nice 3# pompano on south beach. Jim the owner of Psycho Rigs caught a 5# sheepshead down at the inlet. Priscilla caught herself a nice bluefish here in Avon. Isabella with another nice bluefish today. Jeremy caught a pretty 25" speck in the sound. Jennie found a nice bluefish this afternoon. Nice workeveryone, thanks for the photos.
