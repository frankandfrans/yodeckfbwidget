FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 11/26/25


Wind has been picking up today as the day has gone on, and temps are slowly lowering into the fifties for tomorrow, then colder on Friday! We had some good reports today, including small black drum, sea mullet and sharks here in Avon, and scattered sharks, spot, croaker, blowtoads and sea mullet up and down the beach. There were also some blues and a few drum in Buxton this afternoon.

Cooper had some good luck with F&F mullet last night, catching 30" and a 40" red drum! And Marco caught quite a few sea mullet today for dinner! Nice job, guys!!
