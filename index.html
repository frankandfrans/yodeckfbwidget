FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 5/25/2026


We have had all sorts of weather today! Windy, rainy, sunny, all in one! According to the forecast, the rain this morning should be the last of it for a couple of days. Also, it looks like the temps will be consistent, with highs in the upper 70s and lows in the mid 70s to the upper 60s.

We heard of some sea mullet out there today, and we also weighed in some citations! Reece (age 4), Preston, Aubrey, and Jackson got some nice ones. We also heard of a few bluefish on the beach today. The soundside is starting to heat up as well. Travis and Rodney both caught pups on the sound last night. Pretty work, y'all! Capt Rick and his crews have been getting on the sheepshead bite! Lots of yummy dinners ahead!
