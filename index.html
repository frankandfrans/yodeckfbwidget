FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 11/12/25


The wind has slowly decreased and temperatures today have slowly increased...a little. Today we have reports of some sea mullet here in Avon as well as a few puppy drum, but most reports from up and down the beach have consisted of sharks and skates. Jacob had some good family time here on the island and caught a pretty puppy drum! Mullet Man Michael Creech got a cooler full of sea mullet on ramp 38 today, and Benny and Kaylee posed outside the shop with their puppy drum they caught; it was Kaylee's first drum! Way to go! Jarrad and his crew, Clark, Michael, Soren, Owen, Preston, Phil and Daniel all caught some nice sharks, spot and drum this past week! Great work, everyone! Thank you for sharing!
