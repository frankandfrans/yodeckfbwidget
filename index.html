FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 6/18/26


It. Is. WINDY.
The winds will calm down some tonight, but pick back up to 20mph from the SW with some possible storms tomorrow. It's been blowing pretty fierce out there today, so we haven't had many reports - just a couple of bluefish on the beach and some sea mullet. Chester caught lots of mullet on the north side of the island today, including a couple that weighed just over 2lbs! Captain Joey Van Dyke (Fingeance Sportfishing Charters) has been putting folks onto quite a few red drum recently on the water. Nice work, everyone!
