Ever seen the water start “boiling” and wondered what to do next? 👀🌊


That could be a bluefish blitz — and when it happens on Hatteras Island, you do not want to be digging through your tackle bag. Thanks to Kimberly Fazekas for the video.

Watch for:
✅ Birds diving low
✅ Baitfish spraying
✅ Sudden splashes
✅ Slicks or breaking fish near the beach, pier, inlet, or sound

When you see the signs, cast fast, retrieve faster, and hang on. Bluefish are reaction feeders, and hesitation can cost you the bite.
