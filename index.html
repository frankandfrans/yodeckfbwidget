FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 11/23/2025


It was a nice day out today with some decent reports. 34S seemed to be the most productive ramp for sea mullet and black drum. Fishbites and shrimp have been the bait of choice for the mullet. Steve spent the day catching them by the pier with the F&F Mango Sunrise and fishbites. Ethan was using the same bait and fishing at 34 yesterday when he caught a cooler full of mullet.
