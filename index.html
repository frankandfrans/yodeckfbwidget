FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 5/19/25


Its been a pretty day on the island with plenty of sunshine and low humidity. The fishing has been lackluster. Here in Avon, we have heard of lots of croaker, a few sea mullet and a couple of blues. The Point area has had sharks and rays. Hatteras inlet had very little to report. Still getting good reports of puppy drum and blues in the sound. Jef caught this nice big drum last week, it was 47". Capt Rick took his charter out and found lots of puppy drum and blues. We just got fresh mullet in.
