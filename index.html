FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 4/10/26


Today has turned out to be a gorgeous day with a little less wind, but it's taking a lot of weight to hold bottom; 6oz and up seems to be what's needed. We have reports of red drum up and down the beach today, with most reports coming from ramp 34. Tracy sent us a picture of Paul with his 36" red off of ramp 38 this morning, and she said it was one of three that he had caught earlier today, and one of 12 he's caught this week. Chester caught puppy drum on ramps 30 and 34, and Chuck caught a 28" pup on ramp 27 on fresh mullet. Richard, Pete, and Sonny caught some nice pups, too, on fresh mullet! Kurt got a 31" red on ramp 34, and Jonathan caught a flounder in Hatteras Village. Nice catches, everyone! Thank you for sharing!
