FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 7/3/25


The day started out with some rain and thunder, which has since moved on, and it's been a nice day. This morning saw a flurry of blues on most of the island. Sharks seemed to have chewed pretty good last night here in Avon. The beach here in Avon has reported steady action on croaker, along with a few sea mullet. The soundside is still doing well with blues, drum, and trout, esp for those fishing at night. Cody found himself a nice 75" blacktip first thing this morning here in Avon. David and Parker caught some of those blues this morning. So did Rudy and family. Capt Joey Van Dyke out of Hatteras Harbor found blues and trout for his charter.
