FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/27/25


We had some rain on and off today, but it was a productive day. We've had reports up and down the island once again of bluefish, small pompano, sea mullet and some blowtoads here and there. Cut mullet is the bait of choice on two-drop bottom rigs. On the soundside, popping corks and bottom rigs are catching blues, puppy drum and speckled trout.
Captain Joey VanDyke put his charter onto some nice specks and blues today! Great work!
