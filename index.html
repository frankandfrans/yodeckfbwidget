FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 7/28/2025


Another nice day out there, but hot! Fishing has been much more productive in the early morning or evening when it is a little cooler outside and the sun isn't blaring! Adam caught some nice pompano again today, both on the Psycho's Surf Fishing Legs and Eggs rig. Inshore charters did well with blues, speckled trout, spanish, and puppy drum. Captain Rick and Captain Joey VanDyke both put their crews on the fish.

We can get you set up with what you need to catch these spanish and pompano while they're here and the water is clear! We have got plenty of lures, including Hopkins, sting silvers/glass minnows, diamond jigs, and more in stock for the spanish. And a variety of rigs that are great for the pompano, including Psycho's Surf Fishing rigs, which you can check out on our website at https://hatteras-island.com/search.php?search_query=psycho
