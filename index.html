FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 6/2/25

It has been a nice day on the island, mostly sunny, light SE winds with a hint of humidity. The northern beaches had a showing of sea mullet, blues, and a few spanish. The Point had spanish, blues, sharks, and rays. Frisco Beach reported puppy drum, pompano, blues, and spanish. The inlet had some mullet, pompano, and spanish. The Bonner bridge pier had some blues and sheepshead. The sound side is producing some good puppy drum and bluefishing right now. Kai had a nice trout while fishing with his granddad. Brian came across a nice puppy drum soundside. Curtis found some pompano on Physco Rigs down on 55. https://hatteras-island.com/search.php?search_query=psycho. Brandon caught sheepshead on the Bonner Bridge. Capt Joey Van Dyke of the Finegance found his crew a very nice 36" drum. Wendy sent us a photo of a pretty drum caught yesterday.
