FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 5/18/25

It has been a windy day with rough surf. The rough surf has led to slower beach fishing. Those who were willing to move around a bit eventually found fish. In Avon we heard of a few blues , small croaker, and sea mullet. The Point area had sharks and one large drum. The Frisco beach area had some nice sea mullet and croaker. The Inlet had clear water and a good amount of blues on bait. The sound area produced a good number of blues and puppy drum.
