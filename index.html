FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 5/27/26


Another wonderful day on the island, with reports of sea mullet, bluefish, and pompano around today, and puppy drum scattered around last night. Today on the beaches, we heard it was a bit grassy, and there were biting flies around in some places, but that didn't stop fish from being caught. Mack caught a 2lb sea mullet at ramp 55 today, and Jim was down at 55 as well, and caught a 3.84lb pompano. Chris caught a nice bluefish on ramp 30 this afternoon, and Travis sent a photo of a skate caught on the soundside. Thanks for sharing, everyone!
