FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 4/30/26


Another cloudy day with some rain showers today. Reports have been of sheepshead, blues, black drum, and some puppy drum up and down the beach. The water is still pretty turbulent, and it's been taking sputnik sinkers to hold bottom, for the most part.
Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler Charters) had some gentlemen from upstate New York come down to fish the Outer Banks! They had a successful day, catching a haul of sheepshead and bluefish. Today, Chuck caught a 16" sheepshead and a 22" black drum off the beach here in Avon, using shrimp as bait. Nice catches, everyone!
