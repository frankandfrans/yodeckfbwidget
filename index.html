FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 7/30/25


Not sure if the heat ever ends! Another hot one today, with reports up and down the beach of small pompano, some small flounder that were caught and released, and blues and Spanish around Avon this morning. Captain Joey VanDyke of Fingeance Sportfishing has been having some great charters, putting them onto red drum, blues, Spanish mackerel, and albacore. Jim Price of Psycho Surf Fishing took his grandson Lucas out to Ramp 55 today and Lucas got a 2lb 8oz pompano, using softshell crab as bait and the Legs and Eggs rig. We also got a picture from up the beach, Eric with a small Cobia caught in South Nags Head! Thanks for sharing the photos, everyone!
