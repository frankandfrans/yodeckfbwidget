FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 6/26/26


It's been a really warm end to the week and mostly sunny! We got reports of some blues and Spanish up and down the beach, and some pompano have been caught on the south beaches today.
Luke got another nice puppy drum at Ramp 49, once again caught pretty close in to shore on sand fleas. Colton caught and released some needlefish and a nice flounder! And we just had Hunter weigh in his 2lb 4oz pompano. Pretty work, y'all!

Sand fleas have been catching pompano, puppy drum, sea mullet, and more, and we have frozen sand fleas in stock, as well as sand flea rakes to catch your own and use them fresh!
