FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 6/10/26


Today we've had reports of sea mullet and bluefish cruising up and down the beach, as well as some Spanish and small flounder. Frisco down to Hatteras produced some pompano.

Jessica and Annistyn caught some fish soundside today, and Neri and Tiya caught some nice fish today, as well: a sea mullet and a flounder! Nice work!
