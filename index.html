FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 12/28/2025


What a beautiful day out today! It was a great day to take down the outdoor Christmas decor to get ready for the New Year! Although it was a great day to be out there, there were no reports. The wind wasn't bad, but the current was ripping. Adam did well yesterday with some black drum and puppy drum on the Psycho's Surf Fishing. Adam's Black Drum Rigs, made by Psycho's surf fishing, were used. For bait, he used shrimp and Fishbites Electric Chicken Shrimp. https://hatteras-island.com/psychos-surf-fishing-adams-black-drum-rig/?searchid=191226&search_query=adam

Looks like tomorrow the wind is going to pick up quite a bit and then die down a little into Tuesday and the rest of the week. It is also going to get a good bit colder after tomorrow, so hopefully the fishing will be good!
