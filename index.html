FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/5/25


It's been a really nice day today; not so much wind and some good fishing! There are reports of Spanish Makerel, small pompano, bluefish and baby jacks today here in Avon off the beach, including some nice ones caught by Joe!
