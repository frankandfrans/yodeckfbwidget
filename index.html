FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 6/11/26


It's been really blustery out there today; the surf is stirred up, and it's taking upwards of 5 ounces to hold bottom out there. We have reports today of scattered croaker, spot, sea mullet, and puppy drum here in Avon.

Christian caught a nice 29" pup just down the road from the shop, and Reagan has been nailing the spot, croaker, and sea mullet! On the soundside, Captain Frank Hopkins (Easy Going Charters) took out Connor (10), who caught a Bluefish and a smooth dog shark, and Avery (13), who caught her first red drum, measuring 37 inches. They said, “It was a legendary trip.”
