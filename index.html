FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 8/28/25


The ocean calmed down some more today, and we have reports of bluefish scattered around the island, puppy drum and spot in Buxton, drum in Rodanthe last night and this morning, and blues, sea mullet and spot on the Avon Pier. Randy caught a bluefish yesterday on Ramp 38, and Captain Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) had a good charter with Brandon, Sam and Josh from Maine, catching blues and specks, and a blowtoad! Good work!
We got in some more pairs of Xtratufs today, in a few styles and sizes! Stop in and check them out! We are open daily, from 6am-9pm, and you can shop for them on our website at https://hatteras-island.com/boots/
