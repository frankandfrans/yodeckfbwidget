FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 5/19/2026


It has been another beautiful day on the island! Lots of fish caught in the sound today, and a few reports on the beach as well. The folks that headed out on the boats caught some puppy drum, blues, and sheepshead. We had reports of blues and sea mullet on the beach today. Jim Price of Psycho's Surf Fishing also caught a nice sheepshead on his legs and eggs rig.

Robert caught a nice blue today. Rick and Mike caught a couple of nice puppy drum. Alan and Susan had another good trip with Captain Rick. Tim caught a sheepshead, a little over 9 pounds. Captain Rick found its tooth on the deck when he was cleaning the boat later that day!
