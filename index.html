FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 6/12/25

It has been a nice day here on the island, especially if you like catching fish. Working from north to south. The Bonner Bridge has had some good sheepshead fishing, mixed with blues and a few puppy drum. Rodanthe has been covered up with little croaker. Avon, we have had sea mullet, puppy drum, bluefish, and croaker. At night, there have been plenty of sharks as well. Avon Pier has had blues up to 30", along with spanish. The soundside here in Avon has been producing plenty of blues and drum, up to 35" the past few nights. Down at the inlet, we have heard of more pompano, with another citation today, of course, it was on a Psycho rig https://hatteras-island.com/psycho-s-surf-fishing. We have some great customer catches to show off today. Lynette caught a nice 27" drum here in Avon today. Curtis with a citation pompano today, some good eating right there. Luke and Nick found a few trout/released in the sound. Bodie caught a good croaker up near the Tri-villages; his sister, Hazel, and Dad were also busy catching. His other sister, Lola, was doubling them up, nice work fam. Capt Joey Van Dyke of the Fingeance down in Hatteras was busy catching blues and cobia today. Finally, Capt Rick Scarbourgh was finding puppy and blues for his charter. Great work yall, thanks for sharing. We are here 6am-9pm daily. #fishingreport #hookedonfandf
