FRANK AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 7/18/25


It's been a hot one with a steady SW wind in the upper teens. Once we get to Monday, the wind flips North then E and NE the rest of the upcoming week, which should lead to good fishing. Today was fairly slow. More Sheepshead on the Bonner bridge. On the beach here in Avon, we heard of decent shark fishing with some groups getting several and others getting skunked. During the daylight hrs, it's been mainly small spot, croaker, and a few small blues. The soundside has been a little slow as well, with scattered blues and trout.
Steven caught a few spanish on the beach. Marco caught his 1st pompano along with his award-winning smile. Karson found a few bailer Mahi and found his new Sord knife made short work of them. https://hatteras-island.com/sord-fishing-products-knives/?searchid=155920&search_query=sord
