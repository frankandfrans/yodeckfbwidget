FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 8/31/2025


It has been a nice day out, but windy. We heard of blues, sea mullet, and a few flounder today (which were released for tomorrow!) Flounder season opens tomorrow, 9/1, and lasts until 9/14. Get out there and get them while you can. Yesterday, Jimmy got a nice sea mullet just over 16". Unfortunately, we didn't get to weigh this one in, but it was likely a citation!

The soundside has been producing well. Lots of drum reported, many being over 40". James and his buddies went out with Capt Sam Bell with Tightlinez Charters and caught a nice 48" drum and a couple of slot size. Steve was out with Capt Frankie on Easy Going Charters and caught a 44" drum. They are out there, and our local guys can put you on 'em!
