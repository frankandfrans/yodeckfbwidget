FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 5/16/2025


Today has been a little warmer and humid. The wind out of the west brought SSW brought out the biting flies today. We had reports of a few drum in Buxton this morning. Ramp 48 and 49 we heard sharks and a good sea mullet bite. Captain Rick Scarbough has had a good week with some nice sheepshead and drum being landed on his boat.

Looks like tomorrow morning should be nice but the wind picks up again in the afternoon. We open at 6 am and have fresh mullet, shrimp and various frozen baits. Stop in and see us or visit us online at www.hatteras-island.com.
