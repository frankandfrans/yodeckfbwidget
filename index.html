FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 8/14/25


The day started out pretty, but turned rainy and nasty this afternoon and evening. Before the rain came, we had reports of bluefish here in Avon and going all the way down to Hatteras, and some small flounder were caught and released in Hatteras. Our own Randy caught a bluefish this afternoon here in Avon, and Captain Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) had Haden and Joyya on board today and they caught some blues and puppy drum.
