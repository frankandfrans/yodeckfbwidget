FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 10/9/25


It's definitely starting to feel more fall-like with this cool NNE wind, and the ocean is stirred up, which is perfect for the drum. We heard of a good drum bite today on Avon Pier, and of a few bites in Buxton and up in Rodanthe, too. Other than the drum, there are blues, small pompano, and some sea mullet and spot in the surf. On the soundside, there have been some blues being caught. Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) and his charter caught some big blues, and also had a porpoise sighting! Looks like an awesome day!
