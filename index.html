FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 7/24/25


Today has been partly cloudy on and off here in Avon and the ocean has been pretty rough once more, but seems to be taking just a little less weight to hold than yesterday (today seems to be 5oz and up rather than 8 and 10!) We have some reports of croaker and bluefish scattered around the island on the ocean side, with blues, speckled trout and drum on the soundside. Captain Joey VandDyke (Fingeance Sportfishing) sent in a picture from his charter where they got some nice bluefish! Nice work!
