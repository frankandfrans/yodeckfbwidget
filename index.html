FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 10/10/25


It's been mostly cloudy today with a few showers, but the rain hasn't come quite yet.
We have reports today of drum in the surf here in Avon and in Buxton, along with some blues. On the soundside we are hearing reports of bluefish. Chris caught a nice 47" drum today, and Jenn sent us a picture of a 28" drum off the surf as well! We also had Mary send in a photo of Brad and Mason with a 20" flounder they caught and released at the point! Nice catches, everyone! Thank you for sharing!
