FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 5/14/26


It's been breezy today, but very pretty! Today, we heard of bluefish, sea mullet, and some flounder scattered up and down the beaches, as well as some pompano on the south beaches. Mikey just weighed in his 3lb 14oz pompano that was 17" inches long to the fork! The photo doesn't do it justice! Captain Rick Scarborough had a nice day as well, putting his charter (Speck-Tackler) onto some big sheepshead and nice bluefish. Ronnie had a good evening, too, catching a 1lb 6oz sea mullet. Pretty work, everyone!
