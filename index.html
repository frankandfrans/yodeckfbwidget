FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 7/4/26


Happy 4th! It's a hot one out there, but a beautiful summer day. Not too much to report so far, mostly just the smaller sea mullet, spot, and croaker, and there were some pompano down south this morning. Blues and Spanish have been running down on the south beaches as well.
We are closing at 4pm today, but will resume normal operating hours tomorrow morning! We hope everyone has a safe and happy 4th! #fishingreport #hookedonfandf
