FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 11/15/25


Today was beautiful and sunny again, and along with the sharks and skates all up and down the beaches, we heard of blowtoads, sea mullet, and puppy drum here in Avon and in the Tri-Villages, and sea mullet and blues from Buxton to Hatteras. Kurt got a pretty red drum and a bluefish on Ramp 44, and Captain Joey VanDyke (Fingeance Sportfishing) had a great day with his charter on the water today, catching black drum, sheepshead, and bluefish! Pretty work, everyone!
