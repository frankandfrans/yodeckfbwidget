FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 10/6/2025


Another beautiful day on the island, but the recent rain/flooding has these mosquitoes out and about! We are well-stocked on Skeeter Beater. Come and grab some to make your trip out to the water more enjoyable. We heard of some blues, puppy drum, and speckled trout on the soundside today. All we heard from the ocean side was a couple of sea mullet and one nice 43" drum caught this morning.

Brian caught a nice bluefish on the soundside the night before last! Capt Rick also did well today with some puppy drum. Pretty work, y'all!

Not only do we like to hear about your catches, we like to see them too!! If you've caught any fish the past few days, comment them below! You can also email them to us at fandfpics@hatteras-island.com or send them through Facebook or Instagram.

We have our mullet order in for tomorrow, so as long as he catches them, we will have them! We usually get them sometime in the afternoon, around 3-4. But keep an eye here, and we will post when they arrive! You can also sign up for our fresh bait alerts with the QR code. We send out an email directly to you when the bait gets here, so you get alerted.

Until the mullet arrives, we have the following selection of baits: fresh bunker, shrimp, bloodworms, and a large variety of frozen baits! We are open from 6am-9pm daily.
