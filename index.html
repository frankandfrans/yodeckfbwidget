FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 12/6/25


The weather was pretty overcast today, but no rain showed up until late this afternoon. Today we have reports of sea mullet still running up and down the beach, along with some small black drum, trout and flounder here in Avon and blues and flounder down in Hatteras. Fishbites are still the bait of choice for most things, as well as fresh shrimp, which we have plenty of at the shop!
