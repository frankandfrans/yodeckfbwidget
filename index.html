FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 8/13/25


We had a nasty start to the day with some heavy rain and rough thunderstorms, but the sun broke through and it ended up being very pretty! There were reports of blues last night in Avon, but as for today not much was reported after the rain. Captain Joey VanDyke (Fingeance Sportfishing) put his charter onto some nice blues, and Captain Rick Scarborough did the same for his charter! Pretty work!
