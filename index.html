FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 7/31/25


It's already the last day in July, and it went out with a breeze, but it's still pretty hot out. Today on the ocean side we have reports of small pompano scattered about, a bluefish and Spanish bite here in Avon this morning, and some sea mullet down at Hatteras Inlet. Soundside is still producing speckled trout, blues and puppy drum. Captain Joey VanDyke (Fingeance Sportfishing) and his charter had a great day of catching bluefish; Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) put his charter onto some blues, speckled trout, and a cobia! Jim Price (Psycho Surf Fishing), Adam and Steve caught some nice sea mullet this morning down in Hatteras. Pretty work, everyone, and thanks for sharing! The sun has been brutal out there, so make sure you carry some sunscreen, and if you need it, aloe vera!
