FRANK & FRAN'S WEEKLY KIDS' FISHING TOURNAMENT RESULTS & HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 6/19/26


It's been windy, and we had quite a few heavy downpours and thundershowers today. Not the best conditions to be out in, but we did have a report of a pompano being caught on Ramp 48 by Alan, using the F&F Sand Flea Imposter Rig, and Mason caught a 13.5" sea mullet earlier this week! Nice fish, y'all!
