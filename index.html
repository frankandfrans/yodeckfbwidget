FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 5/14/25


Today we've had a good bit of wind from the South and some strong currents out there; people have been reporting that it's taking up to 10 ounces to hold. There were also reports of some slot puppy drum last night here in Avon.
