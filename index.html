FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 9/24/25


The ocean calmed down some today, but so did the fishing. We have scattered reports of smaller bluefish, small pompano, sea mullet, and a report of one puppy drum out there on the ocean side. Soudside has reported blues, some speckled trout, and some puppy drum. Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) had a great charter, catching tons of bluefish and some puppy drum. Nice work! We had a load of fresh cob mullet come in, as well as a few fingers mixed in, so come get 'em while they're here! We also restocked some Z-Man soft plastics and jig heads.
