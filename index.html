FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING / WIND REPORT!


Today was kind of a mess in Avon, with strong winds and some sound side flooding the day started out really slow. Not many folks went out to brave the crappy, windy and cold conditions. We did hear of a few sea mullet, a few small puppy drum and one large drum in Avon. We didn't hear much of anything from the rest of the island other than sharks.

As the day went on, the winds started to diminish. Unfortunately, they will begin to rise as the day goes on tomorrow. Slightly warmer temps tomorrow, though.
