FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 10/5/2025


What a beautiful day after many days of nasty weather! Finally, some sunshine! We heard a few drum off the pier this morning, and a few scattered blues and puppy drum. The sound has had some blues as well. It was a little slow report-wise today.

We got another load of fresh bunker today. Our local commercial fisherman catches them and then puts them in a brine to help firm them up and keep them fresh. It is some of the best bait you can find and the drum love them!
