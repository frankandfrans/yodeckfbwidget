FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 7/9/2025


We have had sunshine, but some pretty rough surf out there today, and it's been difficult to hold bottom. Sputnik sinkers https://hatteras-island.com/sinker-guy-sputnik-sinker/?searchid=153900&search_query=sputnik seem to be the weight of choice today. There are reports of LOTS of croaker scattered around on the ocean side, along with some bluefish heard of here in Avon and in Frisco, along with some scattered sharks. Soundside has been producing some puppy drum, blues, and speckled trout, with the best bite for pups and blues being at night. Captain Sam Bell of Tightlinez charters had a succesful day pulling in some puppy drum. Tommy sent in apicture of Rocco with a nice bluefish from Monday night, and Captain Joey VanDyke of Fingeance Sportfishing had a good charter, getting some blues and trout, and Brad got a nice Spanish this morning!
