FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 6/3/2025


It was a beautiful day to spend outdoors! We had reports of scattered sea mullet, croaker, and blues out there today. Glen got a pretty 3lb 9oz pompano today, along with a few other 2+lb citation pomps. Austin and Thomas got some nice blues out there, and Ricky landed a cobia on the surf! Katie, Brandon, and Lola got in on the action with blues and spanish, from the surf and soundside.

We have also had reports of good action from the sound side, including puppy drum to yearling size drum, bluefish, and a few speckled trout! Rick got a pretty 34" one out there today.

We are loaded up on fresh mullet, shrimp, bloodworms, and a variety of frozen bait. Come and see us from 6am-9pm daily, or check us out online at www.hatteras-island.com. As always, send pictures of your catches to frankandfransavon@gmail.com or through Facebook or Instagram!
