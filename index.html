FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 5/22/2025


Today was another windy day on the island. We had reports of spanish and blues on the Avon Pier and Avon beaches. Spanish and blues on ramp 44. Hatteras reported small flounder, sea mullet, and pompano.

Jim landed a couple pompano at ramp 55, one was unofficially 3 lb 11 oz caught on @psychos_surf_fishing sand flea legs and eggs rig. The other was 2 lb 3 oz caught on psychos_surf_fishing jumbo sand flea legs and eggs rig.

Michael "MulletMan" Creech landed 14 nice sea mullets today down South. Many were 1.25 lbs to 1.75 lbs. All on sand fleas.

We are open 6 am - 7 pm. As we head into Memorial Day weekend, our summer hours go into effect on Sunday and we will be open 6 am - 9 pm.
