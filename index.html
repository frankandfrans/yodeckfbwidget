FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT- 6/6/2026


Pretty warm out there, with a bit of a breeze. Have not heard much out there besides some blues and sea mullet in the surf. Soundside is holding speckled trout, drum, and bluefish.

Popping corks are working very well soundside with everything. To use the popping cork, all you do is tie it onto your line, tie a leader 18 - 24 inches depending on the depth of water you are fishing, add a jig head and soft plastic. Then you cast it out there, and as you reel it in, pop it every 30 seconds to mimic natural bait movement by creating sound and water splashes.
