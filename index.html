FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 10/4/25


Another gorgeous, windy day in the books. The surf is continuing to calm down little by little. We heard of a drum off of Avon Pier today, but other than that, the surf side is still quiet and rough. The sound side continues to produce blues and puppy drum, as well as some larger drum. Bobby caught a 46" red on the soundside last night, and Captain Joey VanDyke and his charter Fingeance Surf Fishing had a good day, loading up on blues and lots of undersized drum. We have fresh bunker on deck, and frozen finger and cob mullet, tuna belly, peeler crabs, fresh shrimp, and plenty of other good baits.
