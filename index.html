FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 5/22/26


We started off the day with some rain showers that stopped in the early afternoon, and the clouds stuck around for the day, but there were plenty of fish around. There are reports of sea mullet and bluefish scattered on the oceanside, and there were some pompano today in Avon. Soundside has been producing puppy drum and bluefish. Darrell and Bert came to weigh in some nice pompano they caught today at ramp 38. Darrell's weighed in at 2lbs 9oz, and Bert's weighed 2lb 3oz. Nice catches, guys!
Amanda and Seth got married on Tuesday, and on the way home, stopped and took some photos at one of their favorite places: Frank & Fran's! Congratulations to you both, and thanks for coming by and sharing!
Brent sent in some pictures of bluefish that Austin caught on Monday at ramp 55. Looks like you guys were on a roll!
