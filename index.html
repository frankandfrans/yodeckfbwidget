FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 11/21/25


Most of the day was overcast and rainy, but despite the unfavorable conditions on the beach, fish were still caught. There were reports of a good sea mullet bite earlier in the morning near the Avon Pier, and puppy drum and sea mullet have been reported throughout the day from north of Avon to Buxton. Jason sent us a picture of his puppy drum he caught, and Micheal "Mullet Man" Creech had two small pups, 16 and 17 inches, bite at the same time on a double bottom rig! Both drum were safely released, but the same can't be said for all the sea mullet he caught! He got a nice cooler full of them throughout the day! Those will make a great meal!
Thanks for sharing, guys!
