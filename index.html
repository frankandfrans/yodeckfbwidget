FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 9/23/2025


A beautiful day out today, but the ocean is still angry! Hopefully, since the wind died down significantly today and will remain low tomorrow, it'll calm down even further. The sound side has been doing well with drum and blues as well!
