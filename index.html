FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 11/7/25


The Anglers Club Tournament wrapped up earlier this evening, and we heard of several more nice fish caught! There were reports of sea mullet and puppy drum up and down the island, as well as some bluefish reported here in Avon and in Buxton. Some small blowtoads have been caught as well. Traci sent us a picture of 8-year-old Brian with his puppy drum here in Avon, and the family caught another small pup as well! Rodney caught a nice puppy drum, too, and Captain Joey VanDyke (Fingeance Sportfishing) and his charter had a successful day landing some sheepshead. Very nice catches, everyone! Thanks for sharing them! Another 500lbs of fresh mullet just hit the deck a little while ago, and we have bloodworms back in stock as well.
