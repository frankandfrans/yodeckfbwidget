Here’s your quick island starter guide 👇
🛻 Beach Driving Permits
Taking your 4x4 out on the sand? Grab your permit online first, then stop by Frank & Fran’s for a FREE self-cert card — just add your permit number and display it.
👉 https://www.recreation.gov/vehiclepermits/249978

🔥 Beach Fires
Beach fires are allowed with a permit, and we’ve got you covered with firewood and the required fire permit. Nothing beats an evening fire on the beach with family and friends.

🎣 Fishing Licenses
Ages 16+ need a North Carolina fishing license — and you can get yours right here in-store. It’s quick and easy with your email and last 4 of your SSN, and the license goes straight to your inbox.

🐟 Fishing Report & Gear
Bluefish are being caught on cut bait and metal lures, and Spanish mackerel are hitting metal lures with fast retrieves. Croakers are biting on shrimp, bloodworms and Fishbites. Pompano are being caught on Electric Chicken Fishbites and sand fleas. Red drum caught on being caught on mullet in the sound especially in the evenings, along with a few speckled trout.

We’re stocked with metal lures, Fishbites, shrimp, bloodworms, sand fleas, fresh bait, rigs, tackle, rods, reels, apparel, gifts, and everything you need for a great week on the island.

Hot rigs right now include Ninja Pompano/Whiting Rigs, Psycho Legs & Eggs, F&F Sand Flea Imposters, OMC Chotty rigs, and our hand tied popper/jig rigs for the sound. Don’t forget the metal lures for blues and Spanish!
