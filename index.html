FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 11/8/25


What a beautiful day and a good day for fishing! We have reports of a little bit of everything: sea mullet, spot, croaker, puppy drum, blues, black drum, and some trout, all ranging from northern Avon to Frisco, and we have heard of puppy drum in Hatteras. Team Specktackler from VA had a great day, catching lots of puppy drum! Jim Price of Psycho's Surf Fishing sent us pictures of Tammy, who limited out on blues at Ramp 48 with Psycho's Cherry Bomb rig, and Adam who caught a 24" puppy drum on Adam's Black Drum rig; and Whitney sent us a picture of her bluefish caught at Ramp 49. Nice catches!
