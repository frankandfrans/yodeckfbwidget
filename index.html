FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 7/22/2025


We have a few nice fish to report today! There have been a few pompano, including Tammy's 2.66lb citation, at ramp 55. We also heard some nice spanish caught at ramp 38 this morning. The sound has been producing some speckled trout and bluefish, plus a few puppy drum.
