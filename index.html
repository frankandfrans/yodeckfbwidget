FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 5/21/2025


The rain cleared off around noon today, and it was a nice afternoon out there! We have had reports of sea mullet, blues, and Spanish. Paul and Sheila got into the blues yesterday morning, and the Spanish in the evening at 38, all on lures. Brian also got a nice blue yesterday on the pier. Pretty fish, y'all!

No rain in the forecast for tomorrow, but warm temps, with the high almost being in the 80s! Looks like it'll be a little breezy with winds 15-25mph out of the west. Make sure to have some bug spray with you, as the west wind will bring the no-see-ums, biting flies, and mosquitoes! We are stocked up on Skeeter Beater, Adios Mo Skiots, Repel, Thermacells, and more.

We will be here 6am-7pm daily until Sunday, 5/25, when our hours switch to 6am-9pm daily! Come and see us.
