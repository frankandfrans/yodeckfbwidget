FRANK AND FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT - 7/18/2026


Been pretty warm out there once again. Thank goodness for that breeze. Heard of some blues, croaker, and some spanish being caught on the surfside.

You can also find these online at https://hatteras-island.com/search.php?search_query=crab
