FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 7/25/25


It's been sunny and hot out there today, and the water has calmed a bit more from the last couple of days. Today we have reports of sea mullet and spot down in Frisco, some blues and croaker in Buxton, croaker here in Avon, and some pompano down on ramp 55. Soundside is still producing red drum, speckled trout, and blues, with a better chance at a bite in the evening and at night.
