FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 6/5/26


Thankfully, the current has slacked off a little more throughout the day, so it's easier to fish. We have reports of blues, speckled trout, and red drum on the soundside, and on the oceanside, there's been scattered sea mullet and bluefish...and even a striper! Josh caught the pictured striper around noon today down in Hatteras, and Emily caught a nice red drum this evening out in the sound! Pretty work, y'all!
