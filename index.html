FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 10/3/25


We started off the day here with some showers, but this afternoon the clouds gave way to a pretty, sunny day! We haven't heard anything from the surf side today, but sound side is still producing blues, puppy drum, and some bigger drum as well! Michael caught a nice 45.5" red today out with Captain Sam Bell and Tightlinez Charters! Captain Joey VanDyke and Fingeance Sportfishing had a good day on the water, too, catching blues and puppy drum. Christopher and his family sent in photos of their catches this week: blues and drum in various sizes, including a 46" red! Thank you, everyone, for sharing! Nice catches!
