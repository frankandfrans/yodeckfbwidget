FRANK & FRAN'S HATTERAS ISLAND FISHING REPORT 7/3/26


Wooooo, it was warm today! Thankfully, there was a slight bit more breeze than yesterday! It sounds like it was typical summertime fishing out there today. Lots of sea mullet, spot, and croaker, but there were a few Spanish and blues around, too, as well as pompano down south. Turner and his family hooked into several croaker and sea mullet while they were here, and Jake caught a nice bluefish! Captain Rick Scarborough (Speck-Tackler) and his charter got into some nice amberjack!

We are open tomorrow morning at 6am as per usual, but will be closing early at 4pm to give our team a chance to spend the holiday with family and friends.
