FRAN AND FRANS FISHING REPORT FOR 5/17/25

The day started out overcast with a few passing sprinkles. It's now clear and the wind is starting to blow out of the SW. The beaches here in Avon reported a few sea mullet and blues. Avon Pier has had a few blues along with sea mullet. Cape Point had a few puppy drum along with good sea mullet. Ramp 49 reported good sea mullet, a few puppy drum, and a couple of pompano. Ramp 55 had sea mullet along with a few pompano. Chase caught a nice 2#3oz pompano on a Psycho rig, Ray found some big rays and a small hammerhead. Den caught a nice puppy drum on ramp 49. Capt Rick Scarbourgh led his client to a nice big sheepshead. Thanks for all the photos. We are here daily from 6am-7pm daily and always online @ www.hatteras-island.com #fishingreport #hookedonfandf
